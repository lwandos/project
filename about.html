Vision Statement

We at Yomelela Public Primary School strive for exellence. We aim to create a learning environment that is
current, creative and proactive where students are encouraged to engage critically in the areas of foundation learning with
the aim of becoming productive participants within local and global communities. We believe that those with the highest
chance of developing this world and making it a better place are those kids that we pay less attention to. We believe that implementing the right tools to these kids
at a young age will make easy for them to adapt to higher education.


Mission Statement

Our mission to provide challenging educational opportunities of a high quality for
our students who are drawn place in and around Khayelitsha, to become
extra ordinary leaders who are competent and confident in their communities and broader society.

As part of ur mission, we aim to:

  • Provide opportunities for holistic devlopment of the students
  • Provide a safe learning environment in which students feel empowered to participate in their education
  • Offer indivindual attention by supporting learners at risk
  • Support and provide opportunities for staff development
  • Encourage parental involvement to bolster the programme of the school
  • Form partenership with community organizations and high schools in support of the school and its students.
